## Study Notes

**Bars 1-4 (Em7b5 → A7 → Dm7):** Minor ii-V-i in D minor — the home key.
Use Locrian natural 2 on Em7b5 (from F melodic minor). On A7, try Mixolydian b9 b13 or harmonic minor V mode. Resolve to D Dorian or Aeolian on Dm7.

**Bars 5-8 (Gm7 → C7 → FMaj7):** Major ii-V-I to F major — the relative major.
Use Dorian on Gm7, Mixolydian on C7. Over FMaj7, Ionian or Lydian both work.

**Bridge:** The B section stays in D minor throughout, cycling the minor ii-V-i before setting up the turnaround back to the last A with Em7b5 → A7.

**Last bar (Em7b5 → A7):** Turnaround back to the top. Use this bar to build tension for the return to the A section.
