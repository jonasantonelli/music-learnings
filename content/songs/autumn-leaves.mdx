## Study Notes

**Bars 1–4 (Cm7 → F7 → BbMaj7 → EbMaj7):** Major ii-V-I to Bb, then moves to EbMaj7.
Use Dorian on Cm7, Mixolydian on F7. Over BbMaj7, try Lydian for color since it's the IV chord relative to F.

**Bars 5–7 (Am7b5 → D7 → Gm7):** Minor ii-V-i in G minor — the home key.
Locrian natural 2 on Am7b5 (from Bb melodic minor). On D7, try Mixolydian b9 b13 or diminished scale. Resolve to G Dorian or Aeolian on Gm7.

**Bridge:** The song reverses the A section — starts with the minor ii-V-i, then resolves through the major ii-V-I.
