## Study Notes

**Bar 2 (Eb7):** bVII7 — a classic backdoor-flavored move out of FMaj7. Use Eb Lydian dominant (from Bb melodic minor) for the #11 color, or Eb Mixolydian for a plainer reading.

**Bars 3–4 (Am7 → D7):** V/ii setting up Gm7, prefaced by its own ii (Am7). The original chart writes altered D7 voicings here (b5b9 / #5#9) — simplified to Am7-D7 for readability. Use A Dorian on Am7, then D altered or D Mixolydian b9 b13 on D7 to capture the tension implied by the original alterations.

**Bars 7–8 (Bbm7 → Eb7):** **Backdoor ii-V to F.** Instead of Gm7-C7 resolving up a fourth to F, the tune borrows iv (Bbm7) and bVII7 (Eb7) and resolves up a half step. Use Bb Dorian on Bbm7, then Eb Lydian dominant or Eb Mixolydian on Eb7. The leading tone here is `Db → C` (the 5 of F).

**Bars 9–12 (Am7 → Dm7 → Gm7 → C7):** Descending root motion in fourths. This is the long approach to the major ii-V-I — Gm7-C7 is the home ii-V, and Am7-Dm7 just extends the cycle backward. Dorian on each minor chord; Mixolydian on C7.

**Bars 13–14 (Em7b5 A7(b9) → Dm7 G7):** Chain of ii-Vs descending by fourths. Em7b5-A7b9 is a **minor ii-V to Dm**, then Dm7-G7 is a major ii-V to Gm. Use Locrian nat 2 on Em7b5, A altered (or A Mixolydian b9 b13) on A7b9, then Dorian/Mixolydian for the next pair.

**Bars 15–16 (Gm7 C7):** The final ii-V before resolving home (or back to top). Standard Dorian/Mixolydian.

**Bar 27 (Bm7b5):** New chord in the C section — voice-leads chromatically down toward Am7 via Bb7 in bar 28. Treat as a brief diversion; B Locrian or Locrian nat 2 both work.

**Bar 28 (Bb7):** Functions as a chromatic approach — half-step descent from Bm7b5 down to Am7. Mixolydian or Lydian b7 both work; the goal is the smooth chromatic landing on Am7.

**Bars 29–30 (Am7 Dm7 | Gm7 C7):** Compressed version of the bar 9–12 cycle, two chords per bar. Same Dorian/Mixolydian approach.

**Bar 31 (F6):** Final resolution — the tune lands on the major 6th instead of the maj7. Bar 32's `Gm7 C7` is the turnaround back to the top (omit on the final pass).
